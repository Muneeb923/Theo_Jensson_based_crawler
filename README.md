# 8-Legged Bio-Inspired Crawler Robot (Theo Jansen Linkage)

A comprehensive mechanical engineering project focusing on the kinematic optimization, parametric CAD assembly, and physical prototyping of an 8-legged walking mechanism.

## 📐 2. Kinematic Optimization & The "Holy Ratios"
The unique walking gait of this crawler relies completely on the specific link proportions discovered by Theo Jansen himself, famously known as the **"Eleven Holy Numbers"**. These precise geometric ratios are crucial because they transform simple rotational driving motion into a smooth, organic, and flat walking stride.

### 🔄 Kinematic Path Analysis
To analyze the mechanics before physical fabrication, motion studies were conducted to map out and evaluate the locus of the foot step-profile:

* **Single Linkage Path:** Visualizing the exact closed-loop path of a single foot to ensure a clean, stable ground-contact phase.
* **Double Linkage Coordination:** Analyzing how two legs interact together to balance weight distribution and maintain a continuous center of mass.
* **Effect of Changing Ratios:** A warning study demonstrating how altering the standard link proportions distorts the foot trajectory and shortens the straight ground-contact line, reducing walking efficiency.
